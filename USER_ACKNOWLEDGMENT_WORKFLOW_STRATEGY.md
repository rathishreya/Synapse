## ğŸ”¬ A/B Testing Ideas

Test these variables to optimize your workflow:

### 1. **Response Timing**
- Immediate vs. 30-min delay vs. 1-hour delay
- Best day of week (Tue-Thu typically win)
- Best time of day (10am or 3pm typically win)

### 2. **Call-to-Action**
- Calendar link vs. Specific times vs. "Reply with your availability"
- "Book a call" vs. "Let's chat" vs. "Quick conversation?"

### 3. **Content Format**
- Plain text vs. HTML email
- Video vs. PDF vs. Interactive content
- Long-form vs. Short-form messages

### 4. **Personalization Level**
- Name only vs. Company + Industry vs. Specific pain point
- Generic template vs. Fully custom message

### 5. **Follow-Up Sequence**
- 2 follow-ups vs. 3 vs. 4
- Daily vs. Every 2 days vs. Weekly
- Same channel vs. Multi-channel
